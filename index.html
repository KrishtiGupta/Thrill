~About us..

We are the team of professional extreme sportsmen. We have been teaching adults extreme sports such as Surfing, Scubadiving, Skydiving, Wingsuiting, Hang gliding and paragliding for more than 5 years. Our coaches are winners and champions of international competitions. Over the years we have developed our own methods and training programs that are based on international best practices.
